Power Outage and Fire Recovery Resources

Strong wind events in California often lead to power outages and wildfires. Below is a list of online resources that provide the latest, up to date information about either.  The following can help you know if your power is about to be shut off, what time that might be, and if there are any fires you should be made aware of. This information will be updated each day as needed.
